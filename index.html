All About US

Swm Technical Works was founded in Kampala, Uganda on 21st November 2017 as a construction company and has successfully defined its objectives and positioned itself as one of the leading organizations in the construction industry.

The head offices and workshops for Swm Technical Works are located in Kisuggu - Muyenga.

Swm Technical Works is registered as a Class "A" Constructor by Ministry of Works and Transport and is registered with Uganda National Building and Civil Engineering Constructors (UNABCEC). Uganda Manufacturers Association (UMA). Uganda National Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Kampala City Traders Association (KACITA), The Public Procurement and Disposal Assets Authority (PPDA), and had its credit many prestigious projects in industrial and civil buildings and is always acknowledge as a reliable and professional construction company by discerning and quality conscious market.

The company has a big capacity to undertake big projects and provides employment to more than 100 permanent staff and over 120 casual labourers stationed on different sites around the country. With strong workforce of professionally qualified construction managers, engineers and supervisors backed up with latest construction equipment and technologically advanced work environment, Swm Technical Works offers customized services to meet the evolving needs of individual customers.
